## Audit Model

- 6 areas x 5 questions = 30 fixed questions.
- Answers: yes/full = 2, partial = 1, no/missing = 0.
- Area score: `raw / 10 * 100`.
- Overall score: average of 6 area scores.
- Levels: Usta `>=80`, Kalfa `50-79`, Sagird `<50`.

## AZ-Specific Value

- Finance area includes POS/Z-report reconciliation, fiscal receipt tracking, monthly expense report, prime cost, and top-10 product margin.
- Compliance area includes AQTA document status, medical checks, delivery temperature regime, cleaning/disinfection logs, and labor/safety process.
- No changing fee/procedure numbers are shown in the UI.

## Output

- Native SVG 6-area bar chart.
- Overall score circle and Ahilik level badge.
- Lowest 3 areas become a priority action plan.
- Zero-score answers become urgent tasks.
- Critical no answers feed the "What do you not know?" management mirror.
