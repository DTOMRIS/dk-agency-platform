## Formula

- Forecast revenue = average monthly revenue x season coefficient.
- Labor budget = forecast revenue x target labor percent.
- Inventory budget = forecast revenue x target food cost percent.
- Weak months are the lowest 3 coefficients; dead month warning applies below `0.80`.
- Strong months are the highest 3 coefficients.
- Ramadan 2026 note is shown for February and March because the effect is mostly time-of-day mix, not monthly total distortion.
