## Formula
- Total sales = food sales + beverage sales + other revenue
- COGS = beginning inventory + purchases - ending inventory
- Food Cost % = COGS / total sales * 100
- Labor Cost % = labor / total sales * 100
- Prime Cost = COGS + labor
- Prime Cost % = Prime Cost / total sales * 100
- Overhead = rent + utilities + insurance + license + marketing + other
- Net Profit = total sales - COGS - labor - overhead
- Breakeven sales = overhead / (1 - variable cost %)
